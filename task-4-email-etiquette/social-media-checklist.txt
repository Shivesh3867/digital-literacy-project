# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (College Students)

##  Do's
- **Think before you post** – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- **Maintain professionalism** – Remember that future employers may view your profile.
- **Protect your privacy** – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
- **Verify information before sharing** – Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.
- **Engage positively** – Communicate respectfully and support constructive discussions.

##  Don'ts
- **Don’t share personal or sensitive information** – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or private details.
- **Don’t post offensive or harmful content** – This can damage your reputation and relationships.
- **Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech** – Stay calm and avoid unnecessary conflicts.
- **Don’t ignore digital footprint** – What you post online can stay permanently.
- **Don’t misuse others’ content** – Always give credit and avoid plagiarism.
