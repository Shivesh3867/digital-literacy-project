## Project Overview
The Digital Literacy Portfolio is a practical project completed as part of my role as a Student Digital Ambassador. The main objective of this project is to develop and showcase essential digital skills that are important for both academic success and future professional growth.  

Through this portfolio, I explored various digital tools and platforms, improved my communication skills, and developed awareness about safe and responsible use of technology. The project includes tasks such as designing an awareness infographic, creating professional profiles, practicing coding, writing formal emails, and understanding cyber safety through real-world scenarios.

---

## Module Breakdown

### Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
In this module, I designed a one-page infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy in a simple and engaging way for first-year students. Using Canva, I focused on three key areas:
- Safe Internet Practices  
- Useful Academic Tools  
- Building a Professional Online Presence  

The aim was to present information in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand format.
